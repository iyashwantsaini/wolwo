# Privacy Policy — wolwo

**wolwo** ("the app") is a wallpaper browser. We respect your privacy and
collect as little data as possible. This document explains what happens when
you use the app.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** wolwo does not have a backend, does not require an account,
and does not include any analytics, ad, or tracking SDKs.

The only data the app stores is on your own device:

| Stored on device | Why |
| --- | --- |
| HTTP response cache (24 hours) | Reduce bandwidth use and speed up scrolling. |
| Image cache | So previously seen wallpapers load instantly. |
| Favorites list | So saved wallpapers persist between launches. |
| Settings (theme, enabled sources, SFW toggle) | To remember your preferences. |

You can clear all of the above at any time from your device's app-info screen
("Storage → Clear data").

## What we share

When you browse wallpapers, the app makes direct HTTPS requests to the
following third-party services. Their privacy policies apply to those
requests:

- **Wallhaven** — <https://wallhaven.cc/help/privacy>
- **Pixabay** — <https://pixabay.com/service/privacy/>
- **NASA Images API** — <https://www.nasa.gov/about/highlights/HP_Privacy.html>
- **Reddit** — <https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy>

These services may receive your IP address and standard HTTP headers as part
of normal network operation. wolwo does not send any other identifiers.

## Permissions the app requests

| Permission | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Internet | Fetch wallpapers from the sources above. |
| Set wallpaper | Apply a wallpaper to your home / lock screen. |
| Storage / Photos | Save a wallpaper to your gallery (only when you tap "Save"). |

## Children

wolwo is not directed at children under 13. The default content filter is
"SFW only" and removes adult / suggestive content from results.
